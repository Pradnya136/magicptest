Magic Phonics Language School

Since then we have been part of the remarkable transition that has made this kind of teaching mainstream around the world.

Why Choose Magic Phonics?

Magic Phonics is the only literacy centre in Coimbatore and Chennai , providing Internationally recognized and worldwide accepted UK method of Phonics. The center started in the year 2015 with a vision to provide quality education with International standards and introducing innovative methods of learning English Language.

Children are not only made confident in reading and writing English but also several activities are taken to make them self-confident speakers too. Reading through Phonics is an amazing interchange to rote learning, where the youngsters learn by repetition. Kids handle the English Language easily when they read in English as well as think and impart in English.

Our Primary English Programme previously known as "lets read" focuses to ensure a strong foundation in English for Primary 3 to 6 students.
